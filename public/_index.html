About NOLADEX

We live, we love, we grow New Orleans

NOLAdex is simply a digital rolodex, a collection of the many talented people of New Orleans.

Here are some ways we think you'd love to use it:

  • find people like you to hangout with
  • hire talent
  • connect digitally to others by creating your own profile

This project was created by generous volunteers who participated in barcamp4 NOLA in one weekend (July 16-17). This site is further maintained by GNO Code.
